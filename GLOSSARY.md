# Glossary

Lockeroom-specific terminology and definitions. Add terms as they come up in rules and SOPs.

---

## Membership & Billing

- **Primary membership** — A member's main membership period; may have secondary add-ons (e.g. boxing, VO2). See [rules/memberships/primary-secondary.md](rules/memberships/primary-secondary.md).
- **Hold** — A full-week (Monday–Friday) pause on a membership with financial and/or session credits. See [rules/memberships/holds-policy.md](rules/memberships/holds-policy.md).
- **Renewal** — The process of a member extending their membership before or at expiry; pipeline stages and assignee logic are in [rules/memberships/renewals.md](rules/memberships/renewals.md).

## Coaching

- **RM** — Results Manager; the coach responsible for a member's overall results and renewal conversation.
- **LC/NS** — Late cancel / No show; session cancellation or non-attendance within the policy window. See [rules/coaching/late-cancel-noshow.md](rules/coaching/late-cancel-noshow.md).
- **WCR** — Whole Client Results; points system for coach performance. See [rules/coaching/wcr.md](rules/coaching/wcr.md).

## Sites

- **Bligh, Bridge, Collins** — Lockeroom gym locations. Site-specific overrides are noted in rule files under "Site Exceptions".
